## MAJOR STRUCTURAL CHANGE: 4-Bracket System (was 2 brackets)

Denmark moved from bundskat + topskat (2 brackets) to bundskat + mellemskat + topskat + toptopskat (4 brackets) in 2026.

## Income Tax

| Component | Rate | Threshold (after AM-bidrag) | Notes |
|-----------|------|---------------------------|-------|
| AM-bidrag | 8% | On gross employment/self-employment income | Unchanged. Does NOT apply to speculative gains. |
| Bundskat | 12.09% | All personal income above personfradrag | Unchanged |
| **Mellemskat (NEW)** | **7.5%** | **Above DKK 641,200** | **New bracket for 2026** |
| Topskat | 7.5% | Above DKK 777,900 | Rate halved from 15% (split into mellem+top) |
| **Toptopskat (NEW)** | **5.0%** | **Above DKK 2,592,700** | **New bracket for 2026** |
| Kommuneskat (avg) | ~25% | All personal income | Varies by municipality |
| Personfradrag | DKK 54,100 | Reduces tax, not income | Up from 49,700 |

### Practical Impact

For most taxpayers earning DKK 600,000-800,000, the effective marginal rate is LOWER in 2026 because the old 15% topskat is split into 7.5% mellemskat + 7.5% topskat with a higher topskat threshold. The mellemskat kicks in earlier (641,200) but at a lower rate (7.5% vs old 15%).

## Aktieindkomst (Share/Equity Income)

| Bracket | Rate | Threshold |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Low rate | 27% | Up to DKK 79,400 per person |
| High rate | 42% | Above DKK 79,400 per person |
| Married couples | Combined DKK 158,800 | Unused threshold transfers between spouses |

**Change from 2025:** Threshold raised from DKK 61,000 to DKK 79,400 (+30%). Significant tax saving for investors.

## Crypto / Speculative Assets

No structural changes for 2026. Same rules as 2025:
- Personlig indkomst under spekulationsbeskatning (statsskatteloven §5)
- NOT aktieindkomst or kapitalindkomst
- AM-bidrag does NOT apply
- Losses only offset same-category same-year speculative gains
- Staking: personlig indkomst at receipt
