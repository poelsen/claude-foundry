# Denmark Tax Data — 2025

Last verified: 2025-01-15
Source: skat.dk, skm.dk

## Income Tax

| Component | Rate | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|
| AM-bidrag (labor market contribution) | 8% | On gross employment/self-employment income. Does NOT apply to speculative gains (crypto, etc.) |
| Bundskat (bottom bracket) | 12.09% | On all personal income above personfradrag |
| Kommuneskat (municipal tax) | ~24-27% | Varies by municipality. Copenhagen ~24.9% |
| Kirkeskat (church tax) | ~0.4-1.3% | Optional (Folkekirken members only) |
| Topskat (top bracket) | 15% | On personal income above DKK 588,900 (after AM-bidrag) |
| Skatteloft (tax ceiling) | 52.07% | Maximum combined state + municipal rate |
| Personfradrag (personal allowance) | DKK 49,700 | Reduces tax, not income |

## Aktieindkomst (Share/Equity Income)

| Bracket | Rate | Threshold |
|---------|------|-----------|
| Low rate | 27% | Up to DKK 61,000 per person |
| High rate | 42% | Above DKK 61,000 per person |
| Married couples | Combined DKK 122,000 | Unused threshold transfers between spouses |

## Kapitalindkomst (Capital Income)

| Type | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| Positive net kapitalindkomst | Added to personal income, taxed at marginal rate. Topskat applies above DKK 48,800 (single) / DKK 97,600 (married) |
| Negative net kapitalindkomst | Deductible at ~33.6% (bundskat + kommuneskat). NOT deductible at topskat rate. |
| Rentefradrag (mortgage interest) | ~33.6% effective deduction rate (declining, legislated toward ~26%) |

## Pension

| Product | Limit (2025) | Deduction | Withdrawal tax |
|---------|-------------|-----------|----------------|
| Ratepension | DKK 63,100/year | Full deduction at marginal rate | Personal income tax |
| Aldersopsparing | DKK 5,600/year (>5yr to retirement); DKK 58,900 (<5yr) | None | Tax-free |
| Livsvarig pension (livrente) | No cap (employer) | Full deduction | Personal income tax |
| PAL-skat (pension return tax) | 15.3% | Annual on pension investment returns | — |

## Aktiesparekonto (ASK)

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Max deposit | DKK 135,900 |
| Tax rate | 17% lagerbeskatning |
| Eligible assets | Listed stocks and equity-based funds/ETFs |

## Crypto / Speculative Assets

| Item | Treatment |
|------|-----------|
| Classification | Personlig indkomst under spekulationsbeskatning (statsskatteloven §5) |
| NOT | aktieindkomst or kapitalindkomst |
| AM-bidrag | Does NOT apply |
| Losses | Only offset same-category speculative gains in same tax year. No carry-forward. |
| Staking rewards | Personlig indkomst at time of receipt (market value at receipt date) |
| NFTs | Likely personlig indkomst (speculative). No definitive SKAT ruling. |

## Mortgage / Realkredit

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Afdragsfri max period | 10 years per 30-year loan |
| Rentefradrag effective rate | ~33.6% (declining toward ~26%) |
| Typical bidrag range | 0.4-1.5% depending on LTV and lender |

## Folkepension

## VSO (Virksomhedsskatteordningen)

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Opsparet overskud rate | 22% (virksomhedsskat) |
| Withdrawal | Taxed as personal income (credit for 22% already paid) |
| Kapitalafkastrate | Set annually by SKAT (~0-4% recent years) |
